Competition Overview

Pencil Royal is a national leadership competition for 32 schools, with students competing in categories like leadership, creativity, and teamwork.

What is Pencil Royal?

Pencil Royal is a prestigious national leadership competition that brings together exceptional students from 32 schools across the nation. This unique platform showcases talent in leadership, creativity, innovation, and teamwork.

  • 32 participating schools nationwide
  • Competitive excellence in leadership
  • Multiple competition categories
  • National recognition and awards

Students compete with peers across the nation, building networks and demonstrating exceptional skills in a prestigious national arena.

Rules

  • Schools must register by deadline.
  • 5 boys and 5 girls per school (maximum 10 students).
  • Voting opens after registration deadline.
  • Top boy and girl from each school advance to finals.

Timeline

Registration Deadline: October 2, 2026

Internal Competitions: October 2 - October 31, 2026

National Finals: November - December 2026
